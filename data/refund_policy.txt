SHOPEASE E-COMMERCE REFUND & RETURN POLICY
==========================================
Version 3.2 | Effective: January 1, 2024
==========================================

1. ELIGIBILITY WINDOW
---------------------
- Standard items: Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery date.
- Electronics: Refund requests must be submitted within 3 days of delivery date.
- Perishables, digital goods, intimate wear, customized/personalized items: NON-REFUNDABLE under any circumstances.
- Items marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase: NON-REFUNDABLE.

2. VALID REFUND REASONS
------------------------
The following are APPROVED reasons for refund:
  a) Item received is damaged or defective upon arrival.
  b) Wrong item delivered (item doesn't match order description).
  c) Item significantly not as described (material difference from listing).
  d) Item never arrived (after 10 business days past expected delivery).

The following are NOT valid for refund (DENIED):
  a) Buyer's remorse / changed mind after delivery.
  b) Item no longer needed.
  c) Found a cheaper price elsewhere.
  d) Did not read product description carefully.
  e) Gifted item no longer wanted.

3. REFUND ABUSE POLICY (CRITICAL — ENFORCE STRICTLY)
------------------------------------------------------
- Any customer who has had MORE THAN 2 approved refunds in the last 12 months is flagged as a HIGH-RISK customer.
- HIGH-RISK customers are INELIGIBLE for refunds on "changed mind" or "not as described" claims.
- HIGH-RISK customers requesting refunds for damaged/defective items require PHOTO/VIDEO EVIDENCE before approval.
- Customers with a prior DENIED refund in the last 6 months are subject to enhanced scrutiny.

4. PRODUCT CATEGORY RULES
--------------------------
ELECTRONICS (phones, laptops, TVs, audio, wearables, cameras):
  - Must be returned in ORIGINAL PACKAGING with all accessories.
  - Must be unused / factory reset (for phones/laptops).
  - Refund window: 3 days from delivery.
  - Software/digital licenses activated: NON-REFUNDABLE.

CLOTHING & FOOTWEAR:
  - Must have original tags attached.
  - Must be unworn, unwashed, and unaltered.
  - Refund window: 7 days from delivery.
  - Undergarments and swimwear: NON-REFUNDABLE.

APPLIANCES:
  - Refund window: 7 days from delivery.
  - Must be unused.
  - Installation damage voids refund eligibility.

BEAUTY & PERSONAL CARE:
  - Sealed/unopened products only.
  - Opened products: NON-REFUNDABLE.
  - Refund window: 7 days.

SPORTS & FITNESS:
  - Refund window: 7 days.
  - Must be unused and in original packaging.

KITCHEN & HOME:
  - Refund window: 7 days.
  - Used items: NON-REFUNDABLE.

BAGS & ACCESSORIES:
  - Refund window: 7 days.
  - Must be unused with tags attached.

5. PAYMENT METHOD REFUND TIMELINES
------------------------------------
- Credit Card: 5–7 business days to original card.
- Debit Card / UPI: 3–5 business days.
- EMI: Converted to credit; remaining EMIs cancelled. No cash refund.
- Cash on Delivery (COD): Refund issued as store credit or bank transfer (5–7 days).
- Store Credit: Immediate.

6. TIER-BASED PRIORITY
-----------------------
- Platinum customers: Priority processing (24–48 hours). Benefit of doubt on first dispute.
- Gold customers: Standard processing (3–5 days). One courtesy exception per year.
- Silver customers: Standard processing (3–5 days). No exceptions.
- Bronze customers: Standard processing (5–7 days). Strict policy enforcement.

7. ITEMS INELIGIBLE FOR REFUND (ABSOLUTE)
-------------------------------------------
The following can NEVER be refunded regardless of circumstance:
  - Perishable food items
  - Digital downloads, software licenses, gift cards
  - Intimate apparel, swimwear, earrings (hygiene reasons)
  - Customized / personalized products
  - Hazardous materials
  - Any item explicitly marked "Non-Returnable" on the product page

8. REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION
---------------------------
For all approved categories, the customer must provide:
  - Order ID
  - Reason for refund
  - For damaged/defective: Photo or video evidence uploaded within 24 hours of request.

9. DISPUTE ESCALATION
----------------------
If a refund is denied, the customer may escalate to a human supervisor within 48 hours.
The supervisor's decision is final and binding.

10. POLICY AMENDMENTS
----------------------
ShopEase reserves the right to update this policy at any time.
Policy in effect at time of purchase governs the transaction.
